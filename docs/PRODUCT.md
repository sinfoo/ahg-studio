# AHG Studio — Product Context

> Separate product from AHG Unlocker. Same brand family (AHG), different audience and register.

## Product Purpose
AHG Studio ("Screen & Optimize") is a desktop screen-recording and video-compression app. It pairs an OBS-class capture/output pipeline (scenes, sources, audio mixing, configurable encoders, up to 144 FPS, high bitrate) with a HandBrake/compressO-class compression studio (shrink recordings dramatically with little to no perceptible quality loss). Record, then optimize, in one tool.

## Users
Everyone who records a screen and cares about the result: enterprises (training, demos), schools and professors (lectures, tutorials), doctors (procedure capture, telehealth notes), creators and gamers (gameplay, streams). Skill levels span complete novices to power streamers. The shared expectation is **premium, trustworthy, fast** — it should feel like professional software regardless of who opens it.

## Register
product — the design serves the task. Earned familiarity over novelty. The tool disappears into recording and compressing.

## Brand Tone
Pro-neutral premium. Confident, calm, precise. Tinted near-black graphite neutrals, one restrained teal-cyan accent, generous whitespace, sharp typography. NOT gamer (no RGB, no neon, no violet glow). NOT corporate-sterile (no flat blue SaaS template). Think the quiet authority of DaVinci Resolve / Linear / Arc, applied to recording.

## Anti-References
- No OBS-reflex aesthetic (battleship gray + a lone red dot as the only color).
- No gamer cues (RGB, neon, cyberpunk borders, glow spam) — that's AHG Unlocker's lane, not this one.
- No generic blue SaaS dashboard / hero-metric template.
- No glassmorphism-by-default, no gradient text, no side-stripe accent borders.

## Strategic Principles
1. Premium for ALL audiences. A doctor and a gamer must both trust it on first open.
2. Dark-first, but light mode is first-class (bright clinics, lecture halls, offices).
3. Powerful like OBS, but calm. Density is organized through one consistent dock language + progressive disclosure, never clutter.
4. "Screen & Optimize" is the north star: recording and compression are equal halves, not an afterthought.
5. Red means recording. Reserve it. Teal carries primary actions and selection.
6. Performance is a feature: smooth motion (150-250ms), no decorative choreography outside the first-run splash.
